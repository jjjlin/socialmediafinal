Tamkang University Jin Shaow-
Creative and Singing Contest

Rules of Jin Shaow Contest:

  • Participant： Colleges and universities students (including accompaniment)
  • Rules： The competition is divided into preliminary and final, after the first round selection of the winner into the final (the final admission group is still determined by the judge)
  • Date and Venue:
    1. Preliminary → solo, sing group: March 25 (Sat.) 10:00 Tamkang Music Hall at Tamkang University (Tamsui Campus)
    2. Preliminary → Songwriters' group: March 26 (Sun.) 12:00 At Tamkang Music Hall at Tamkang University (Tamsui Campus)
    3. Final → May 5 (Fri.) 17:30 at Tamkang University activity center (Tamsui Campus)
