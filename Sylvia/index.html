The Red House Market For Artists & Designers

Information : I believe that many people who have been to Ximending will have seen the Ximen Red House. There may be nothing special on weekdays, but the holiday will be very lively. More than one hundred stalls will sell Taiwanese local handicrafts, such as cloth bags and leather goods. If you take a closer look, there may be unexpected gains!

Address : No. 10, Chengdu Road, Wanhua District, Taipei City

Business Hours : Saturday 14:00-22:00, Sunday 14:00-21:30
